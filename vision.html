meshXfoundation is designed to seamlessly integrate with your existing enterprise infrastructure. Our platform enhances your current workflows without disruption, allowing you to leverage existing investments while evolving your data management capabilities for future growth and innovation.

The problem with modern data: Two thirds of enterprise data is left untapped (IDC)

The current stack reinforces existing data silos, leading to the increase in separation, complexity, expensiveness and general poor quality, and this all leads to massive losses in data utilization

The top barriers to using data:

01.Data is not usable
Organizations often struggle to make raw data usable due to inconsistent formats, poor quality, and lack of integration, reducing trust and completeness.
02.Managing the storage of collected data
The exponential growth of data requires scalable, secure, and cost-effective storage solutions, posing a significant management challenge.
03.Ensuring that needed data is collected
Collecting the right data amidst vast amounts of irrelevant information and aligning with subject matter expertise is crucial yet difficult for effective decision-making.
04.Ensuring the governance of collected data
Implementing robust governance policies to ensure data accuracy, privacy, and compliance is essential but challenging for many organizations.
05.Making the different silos of data available
Breaking down data silos, vendor locks, formats, to enable seamless access and integration across the organization remains a major obstacle.
